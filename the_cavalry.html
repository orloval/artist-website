The Cavalry

2024, 16 min, HD

Director, Camera, Edit by Alina Orlov


Statement

In the film, ‘The Cavalry’, the director travels to Israel, where she was raised, during September 2023 to film the once-fueled public protests against the Israeli government. During that time the director is given access to a secretive section of a police unit, the cavalry, where she documents the desensitization of the imported Friesian horses. The film consists of documentation contrasting horses in a rural setting, the West Bank, and an Israeli police unit.
